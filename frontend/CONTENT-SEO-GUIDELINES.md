# Content SEO Guidelines for ectsyawo.com

## For AI-Powered Search Optimization

This guide ensures all content is optimized for modern search engines, including AI-powered search tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's SGE (Search Generative Experience).

---

## üìù Writing Content for AI Search

### Core Principles

1. **Write for Humans First**
   - AI models are trained on human-preferred content
   - Natural, conversational tone wins over keyword stuffing
   - Focus on being helpful and informative

2. **Demonstrate Expertise**
   - Cite specific experiences and outcomes
   - Include credentials and qualifications
   - Show, don't just tell (e.g., "trained 500+ farmers" vs "experienced trainer")

3. **Be Comprehensive**
   - Cover topics thoroughly in one place
   - Answer related questions
   - Provide context and background

4. **Use Clear Structure**
   - Descriptive headings (H1 ‚Üí H2 ‚Üí H3)
   - Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
   - Bullet points for lists
   - Clear topic sentences

## üéØ Keyword Strategy

### Target Keyword Placement

**Primary Keywords (Use 3-5 times per page):**
- Page title (H1)
- First paragraph
- One subheading (H2 or H3)
- Meta description
- Naturally throughout content

**Secondary Keywords (Use 2-3 times):**
- Subheadings
- Body paragraphs
- Image alt text
- Link anchor text

**LSI Keywords (Latent Semantic Indexing):**
Include related terms naturally:
- Primary: "food systems expert"
- LSI: "food security," "sustainable agriculture," "nutrition science"

### Example - Good vs Bad

‚ùå **Bad (Keyword Stuffing):**
> "As a food systems expert, I provide food systems consulting. My food systems expertise helps organizations with food systems transformation."

‚úÖ **Good (Natural Integration):**
> "As a food systems expert specializing in consumer nutrition, I help organizations transform their approach to sustainable agriculture. My work focuses on practical solutions for food security challenges."

## üìä Content Structure for AI Understanding

### Blog Post Template

```markdown

[150-180 characters for SEO]

## Introduction
- Hook: Engaging opening question or statement
- Problem: What issue does this address?
- Promise: What will readers learn?
- Include primary keyword naturally

## Main Content Sections

### H2 Section Headings (Use Questions When Possible)
- Why is [topic] important?
- How does [topic] work?
- What are the benefits of [topic]?

### Body Content
- 300-500 words per section minimum
- Include examples and case studies
- Use bullet points for readability
- Add internal links to related content

## Practical Takeaways
- Actionable bullet points
- Steps readers can implement
- Resources for further learning

- Summarize key points
- Call to action
- Link to related articles
```

## üì± Mobile-First Writing

### Keep It Scannable
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- Frequent subheadings (every 200-300 words)
- Bullet points over long lists
- Bold key terms (sparingly)

### Sentence Length
- Average: 15-20 words
- Mix short (5-10 words) and longer sentences
- Avoid run-on sentences
- One idea per sentence
